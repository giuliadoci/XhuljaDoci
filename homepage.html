What about me?
Born in Kalamata (Gr) the 14.10.1996, I came to Italy at the age of five. In high school I studied arts and applied arts and that helped me to fall in love with graphics and photography. Those became two of the most important things in my life. Not just a hobby or something to do when I have nothing else.
So I decided to open my personal website, like a portfolio, to show the stuff that I have done in the past years and that I am doing already. Hope you enjoy!
